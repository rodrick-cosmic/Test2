about

I am a 22-year-old, third year student studying Digital Arts at the University Of Witwatersrand. I aspire to one day become a professional game designer and I have a passion for all things digital media, music and film. I spend most of my day, experimenting with software mainly Krita, Blender, Photoshop and of course HTML, CSS and JavaScript. I enjoy coding and the challenge of learning something new everyday. And even more so, I enjoy creating art in whatever capacity.

To create is to live

Part of why I chose this degree and by extension, this course, is I believe that digital art is the final frontier in creative endeavours. Especially in contemporary society. I want to make the space a much more inclusive one where creatives of all shapes, colors,creeds and beliefs can have an equal chance at succeeding. Additionally, I want to make the world a much more tolerable place and I think art is one way to do that because it is able to transcend almost any human imposed boundary.
